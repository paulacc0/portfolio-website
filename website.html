Projects
Faeremin

University project. Touchless instrument based on a theremin for people that cannot play a regular instrument. Me and my partner were fascinated by the idea of a theremin so we decided to create an alternative version of it. With the help of a couple of ultrasonic sensors, 2 Arduino UNOs communicating with each other and a bunch of other components, we were able to create the instrument in the video shown on the left.

Whack-A-Mole

University project. Arduino UNO LED Whack-A-Mole. This was my first ever Arduino project. It was formative but I loved it so much I put more effort in it than in the mandatory one. This must be one of my favourite projects so far. Even if it was a simple game, it definitely did not seem like it at the time but I still enjoyed every second of it. The only thing that stopped me was the battery not being able to handle so many components.

This Arduino UNO setup has 10 LEDs, 1 shift register, 1 piezo, 1 servo motor, 1 potentiometer and 1 LCD screen, 3 buttons and over 10 resistors.
