Faeremin

University project. Touchless instrument based on a theremin for people that cannot play a regular instrument. Me and my partner were fascinated by the idea of a theremin so we decided to create an alternative version of it. ... With the help of a couple of ultrasonic sensors, 2 Arduino UNOs communicating with each other and a bunch of other components, we were able to create the instrument in the video shown on the left.

Whac-A-Mole

University project. Arduino UNO LED Whack-A-Mole game. This was my first ever Arduino project, a modification of the game "Whac-A-Mole", a 2-player reflex game where the aim is to press a button when any of your colour LEDs lights up. ... If you manage to press the button in time, you will score a point. The first player to reach 10 points will be the winner and the game will end.

Please watch the video on the right to see a demo of how the game works.

This Arduino UNO setup includes:

 1 Liquid Crystal Display Screen
 1 shift register
 10 LEDs
 3 10k Ω resistors
 10 560 Ω resistors
 1 servo motor
 3 buttons
 1 piezo buzzer
 1 potentiometer
